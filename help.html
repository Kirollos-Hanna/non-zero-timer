FAQ

How do I use this extension?

  1. Set a time limit (default is 1800 seconds or 30 minutes).
  2. Write down what you want to do when the time limit is reached.
  3. Once the alert shows up, try to do something as big or as little as you like. Just make sure to have a non zero moment within this time.

I clicked on "prevent this page from creating additional dialogs", how do I enable the alerts again?

  1. Right-click on the Non Zero Timer extension.
  2. Click on manage extensions.
  3. Disable and then enable the extension.

(Note: some data may be lost)
